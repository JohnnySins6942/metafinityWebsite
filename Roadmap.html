Roadmap

A look at Metafinity® for the next two years

Pre-Phase (Current)

2021-2022


- Inception of Metafinity®
- Launch of our website
- Launch of our social medias
- Beginning development of Metaverse System
- Core team formation
- Creating marketing strategy
- Establishing plans and vision

Phase 1 (In development)

July 2022 - August 2022


- Private NFT Presale
- Public NFT presale
- Launch of Pre-Metaverse system

Features:

- Ability to visit your land

- Ability to visit other's lands

- Only accessible on PC

- Introduction of universal land price estimation system

Phase 2

January 2023 - Febuary 2023

- Beta release of the Metaverse system

Features:

- Gather and transfer resources

- Build on land

- Communal lands

- Questing and ranking system

- Begin development of native cryptocurrency to be used within Metafinity®

Phase 3

July 2023 - August 2023

- Release of native cryptocurrency system
- Beginning development of in game NFTs system
- Introduction of new sets of NFTS for characters
- Introducing new NFT Collection of 25,000 Land
- Begin development of an alternate reality mobile app

Phase 4

2024+

- Full release of Metafinity®
- Release of VR version of Metafinity®
- Release of mobile app

About Us

Metafinity® is a futuristic concept for a metaverse, a space between virtual and physical reality in which real-world real world applications can be realized in a virtual environment. These environments represented by NFTS can be owned, bought, sold, and in the near future deeply customized.
