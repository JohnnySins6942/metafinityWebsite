About Us

Mission Statement

Our future is virtual. The information age is accelerating technology at an unprecedented speed, as we see more and more science fiction realised. Already today many of us live half online, and with every passing year the fantastic sci-fi metaverses of Ready Player One and Snow Crash seem less far fetched.


There’s a long road ahead of us, as a new virtual future slowly becomes non-fiction, but it may not be as long as many think. Metafinity® believes that carving out the foundations of this world may be one of the most important ongoing technical developments of the next decade, and beyond.


Metafinity® aims to bring people and technologies together by creating a robust location-based games and technologies platform, which supports strong virtual communities and economies. Metafinity® will be about people and the virtual representations of places which bring them together. For this reason, we believe it’s critical to be transparent about our goals, plans, and approach with our community and followers from an early stage.
