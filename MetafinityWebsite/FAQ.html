FAQ

What is Metafinity?

Metafinity® is a futuristic concept for a metaverse, a space between virtual and physical reality in which real-world real world applications can be realized in a virtual environment. These environments represented by NFTS can be owned, bought, sold, and in the near future deeply customized

How much will it cost to purchase a Metafinity® NFT?

Our mint price for each NFT will be $50 USD or around 0.02 ETH

How many NFTS will there be?

There will be 25,000 NFTS upon mint, which means 25,000 available tiles of land for people to buy.

How will different Biomes work?

The tiles of land will be split up into 5 different biomes, which each will have 5 sub-biomes of their own. These biomes will include grassland, frozen-grassland, desert, mountain, and alien. There will be 5000 tiles of each biome and 1000 tiles of each sub-biome.

How will resources work?

Each individual piece of land will have randomly generated resources upon mint. These resources can be harvested and collected by players to build on their land.

How can I visit other people's lands?

You'll be able to visit other people's lands much like how you visit a website on the internet. Our vision is to make it as accessible as possible, with the paste of an address and the click of a button.

How will building on my land affect it?

Building on your land will appreciate it's value and will give you different utilities depending on the type of building you build. From decor to resource generation, the possibilities are endless.

How can I own land in Metafinity®?

Purchase an NFT from our collection and you'll be granted ascess to phase 1 of Metafinity! You can resell, trade, or buy more NFTS whenever you want.

Will I be able to own multiple pieces of land?

Yes you will, and we encourage it with our resource transfer system.

How will wildlife on my land work?

Wild life is randomly generated upon mint of your land. You only have a limited amount and only breeding will grant you more animals on your land. Be sure not to kill your animals too early!

How will NPCS work?

NPCS will visit and stay on your land once you have met certain requirements. This area of the Metafinity® is still very much in development so stay tuned for more details surrounding NPCS!

How will in-game Items work?

Most in-game items will be in the form of NFTs that can be sold and traded to other players on the marketplace we are currently developing!

Are resources on my land finite?

Resources will not be finite, and will regenerate over time. If you've chopped down a tree or demolished a deposit, it will be respawned somewhere on your land after a period of time!

Where do I go to purchase an NFT?

We haven't released our collection yet! Stay tuned for updates, but we plan on releasing our collection somewhere during the summer between July 2022 and August 2022.

Where do I go for more questions?

You can join our discord server and message anyone on our Dev team for information!
