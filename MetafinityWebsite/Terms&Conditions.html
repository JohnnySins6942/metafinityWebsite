Introduction


Metafinity® is a futuristic concept for a metaverse, a space between virtual and physical reality in which real-world real world applications can be realized in a virtual environment. These environments represented by NFTS can be owned, bought, sold, and in the near future deeply customized.

Metafinity® will be a digital and virtual world which will then set a stage on which a much wider set of activities besides world-building can take place. We are undertaking this massive project in five stages.


Stage 1


In Phase 1 (Stage 1), we will launch our Website, introducing our social media sites and begin development of our metaverse system


Stage 2


In Phase 2 (Stage 2), we will launch our NFT Collection of 25,000 tiles of land and our pre-metaverse system where users can interact with their land and other people's lands.


Stage 3


In Phase 3 (Stage 3), we will be launching the beta version of Metafinity®. Users will be able to gather and transfer resources to other users, and begin terraforming and building on their lands. We will also be introducing a ranking system for land to determine how valuable each tile is. Not only so, but we will also be beginning development of a native cryptocurrency system that can be used to settle transactions within Metafinity®


Stage 4


In Phase 4 (Stage 4), we will be our native cryptocurrency system, and begin developing an NFT system for characters/avatars. We will also begin development of our in game NFT item system with our own native NFT marketplace for said items. We will be introducing another collection of 25,000 tiles of land during this period, as well as begin development on an alternate reality mobile app for your land.


Stage 5


In Phase 5 (Stage 5), we will be fully releasing Metafinity®, which will include the transfer from PC applications to Virtual reality applications. We will also be fully releasing our mobile alternate reality app.

Part B - Buying and Selling Virtual Land in Metafinity


A key aspect of the Metafinity® platform will be the ability for registered users to buy and sell land tiles (NFTS).

Each tile of Virtual Land/NFTs will be a virtual equivalent to a real-world measurement of approximately 256mX256m, although they may not all be of exactly identical size. The size and positioning of the Virtual Land is established and controlled by us in our sole discretion.

The first-sale of each tile of Virtual Land/NFTs will be based on a value that we assign to it in ETH. Future sales between registered users will be struck at a price agreed between those users.

If the registered user who holds the rights in any Virtual Land/NFTs (Seller) wishes to sell that Virtual Land/NFTs, it can place the land/NFTs for sale on the designated NFT marketplace. Other users can bid on that Virtual Land/NFT, and the successful buyer (as determined by the Seller) will become eligible to take ownership of the Virtual Land/NFT (Buyer), subject to paying the agreed price to the Seller for the relevant Virtual Land/NFT, and payment to us of the relevant transaction fee (2.5%) (Transaction Fee).
