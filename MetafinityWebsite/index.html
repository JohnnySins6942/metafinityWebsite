Metafinity®


Bringing us one step closer to the future


Metafinity® is a futuristic concept for a metaverse, a space between virtual and physical reality in which real-world real world applications can be realized in a virtual environment. These environments represented by NFTS can be owned, bought, sold, and in the near future deeply customized.

A virtual metaverse is inevitable and Metafinity® is the beginning of this exciting future. Phase 1 is now in development and represents the central global body that aims to determine ownership of digital assets and property inside this futuristic virtual metaverse.

Built on the Unity Engine, we have created a plethora of new worlds, allowing people to claim ownership of Virtual Land in the form of tiles. It is our vision that Virtual Land will increase in value over time based on demand, biome, resources and earning potential, much the same way that physical land does, so be sure to claim your own land in key locations around the world early on!




Metafinity® Vision


We aim for Metafinity® to be the beginning of the Earth's fully immersive virtual reality similar to "The Matrix" or "Ready Player One" and we are so excited to engage this journey together. We plan on rolling out phases over coming months and years that will work toward this final goal and will also keep our community interested and interacting with the concept from the beginning (as opposed to having to only wait for one final end product). Imagine owning land and hosting a vitual concert or even your starting your own virtual business? It may be closer than you think!



Purpose


Our long term goal is to create more purpose and utility for land inside Metafinity® and make our game experience more interactive, increasing the amount of things you can use your land for, such as building virtual structures, going on quests, networking, and being a part of a growing community. We are exploring a number of options, including ways users may be able to permit other users to rent or build on their land, and right now before our phase 1 launch, you can earn a chance to win a plot of free land by being one of the top inviters of people to our discord server!
